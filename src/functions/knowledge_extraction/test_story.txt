Pete Prisco of CBS Sports has released his Week 4 NFL picks, predicting a close victory for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers over the Philadelphia Eagles in a matchup of undefeated teams. He also forecasts the Kansas City Chiefs to defeat the Baltimore Ravens, handing them another loss. Additionally, Prisco anticipates the Green Bay Packers to significantly outperform the Dallas Cowboys, highlighting a potential 'Micah Parsons revenge game' narrative.
